Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

Aura Walls AI respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

Aura Walls AI is designed to collect as little data as possible. We do not require you to create an account. The app stores your preferences locally on your device. This data never leaves your device.

AI Generation

When you use the AI wallpaper generator, your text prompt is sent to our server which forwards it to a third-party AI image generation service (Stability AI). We do not store your prompts on our servers.

Photos and Media

When you choose to save a wallpaper, the app requests permission to access your photo library. We only write images you explicitly choose to save. We never read, scan, or upload your existing photos.

Analytics

We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs. We do not collect usage analytics, crash reports, or device identifiers.

Subscriptions

In-app purchases are processed entirely through the App Store. We do not collect or have access to your payment information.

Third-Party Services

The app uses images from Unsplash for the curated wallpaper collection. When you browse wallpapers, your device connects to Unsplash image servers.

Childrens Privacy

Aura Walls AI does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is rated 4+ and does not contain any age-restricted content.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. We do not display advertisements.

Your Rights

Since all your data is stored locally on your device, you have full control. You can delete all app data by uninstalling the app.

Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact
